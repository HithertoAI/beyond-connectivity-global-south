Principle One: Community Ownership as a Minimum Standard

What community ownership means

Ownership in the context of connectivity must transcend simple property rights. It is defined by the community’s collective capacity to govern, sustain, and evolve its own digital infrastructure without reliance on external profit-driven extractive entities.

This involves a paradigm shift where infrastructure is viewed not as a product sold to a consumer, but as a shared utility managed by the users themselves. Ownership encompasses the physical hardware, the logic of the network, and the fiscal surpluses generated by its operation.

verified Standard 01

Asset Control

Permanent collective control over the physical layer and intellectual property of the network stack.

verified Standard 02

Local Governance

Decisions regarding pricing, expansion, and policy are made through a democratic, consensus-based community framework.

verified Standard 03

Revenue Circularity

All financial surpluses are mandatorily reinvested back into the local infrastructure or community social funds.

The nexus mesh model

The Nexus Mesh Model offers a decentralized alternative to traditional spoke-and-hub architectures. By distributing the "intelligence" of the network across every node, we eliminate single points of failure and central control.

  • Recursive network topology that adapts to local geography.
  • Incentivized node hosting for individual community members.
  • Auto-scaling capacity based on real-time neighborhood demand.

Legal and regulatory preconditions

Implementing ownership requires more than just hardware; it requires a protective legal wrapper. Without specific regulatory recognition, community-owned networks remain vulnerable to hostile takeovers or predatory legal challenges.

Legal Structures

The use of Multi-Stakeholder Cooperatives (MSC) or Public Benefit Corporations (PBC) is recommended to lock in community purpose.

Spectrum Rights

Advocacy for "Secondary Use" spectrum licenses is critical to ensuring local networks have the airwaves necessary to function.
